What is the difference between Synthetic Hair, Human Hair and Remy Human Hair?

Synthetic hair
Beauty Works synthetic hair is manufactured to act and feel like human hair and can even be styled and heated up to 180c in moderation. Recognised as the best quality heat- friendly synthetic fibre, our synthetic hair extensions allow women to change and style the hair as desired. Synthetic hair usually lasts around 8-10 weeks depending on the aftercare.

Human Hair
Beauty Works Human hair is 100% human hair and can last up to 3 months depending on aftercare. Human hair has the cuticles removed which means the life time of the hair extensions are shorter compared to Remy hair extensions. Human Hair extensions are ideal for clients who want hair extensions for a short period of time.

Remy Human Hair
Beauty Works Remy Human Hair is 100% Human Hair. Remy hair is specifically processed to ensure that the protective layer of the cuticles remain intact and are facing in the same direction from root to tip. Ensuring natural shine and vitality allowing the hair to behave tangle-free throughout its lifetime. Remy Human hair can last around 6 – 9 months depending on aftercare. Please note: Styling and Heating all of Beauty Works products can damage the condition of the hair and reduces its life-span.

BEAUTY WORKS HAIR SOURCING

Where is Beauty Works hair extensions from?

Beauty Works source raw human hair extensions from all over the world. Beauty Works hair extensions is part of the largest manufacturer of human hair in the world and work with factories both in India and China to manufacture our production line. This allows us to deliver the highest quality hair at the best price possible. Our partner’s source our raw hair from Malaysia, Vietnam, Mongolia, India and China, depending on the product range this is than manufactured to our specifications before it arrives at Beauty Works.

Is Beauty Works hair extensions ethically sourced?

We source our hair straight from verified distributors, who depend on their long-standing reputation for ethical sourcing and quality hair. Human hair is donated, sold, or exchanged fairly and ethically in the region its sourced. Selling hair is a supplementary source of income for women, which does not require any labour.

How is the hair sourced?

Our factory partners source hair from regions all over the world which are renowned for women donating healthy, strong and long hair lengths. Hair is never chemically treated and grows to lengths up to 32”. The majority of human hair used in extensions comes from India and Asia. Religious believes in Hindu religion believe sacrificing hair is a sign of devotion. Daily pilgrimages to temples such as the Venkateswara Temple in Tirumala, India, where they shave/tonsure their heads in a ritual of devotion. In Asian communities’ women donate their ponytails for a fair value, the hair is willingly donated and cut at the ponytail, leaving the donor with a bob length hair style. The ponytail bundles are than sorted into length and size and exported to our factories where the production of hair extensions starts.

Beauty Works Factory

With over 30 years in the hair extension industry, our factory partners are globally recognised as the leaders in human hair development. The team consists of over 1000 employees whom are skilled at sewing, colouring and crafting to produce quality hair extensions to Beauty Works specifications. From sourcing materials to arranging production, our factory partners strive for excellence every step of the way. Our hair is sourced directly from verified distributors who have a long-standing reputation for ethically sourcing quality human hair. All hair is sold or exchanged freely and fairly.
